Our Core Values

Transparency

We stand with customers' freedom and choice. We assist customers in migrating to better service from us if someone else can serve our customers better than us.

Open code policy

We believe anyone can do value creation. Hence we will allow anyone else to modify and use our code for rolling out better services than us.

Innovation first

We create long-term value for customers by innovation instead of maximizing our profit.

Distributed control

We believe the Internet has to be distributed and independent of anyone's control.



We are problem solvers. not control freaks.

Our Team

We are a team of engineers with a collective passion for building tools to help developers solve their biggest challenges
